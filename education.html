SK Bandar Sungai Buaya (SKBSB)
I went to SKBSB from 2010 until 2015. It is where I grew up and learn as a child. I was also a prefect and elected to be the head prefect in Year 6. Alhamdulillah, thanks to the dedicated teachers that have taught us, I got 5As for my UPSR.

SM Sains Hulu Selangor (SEMASHUR)
I first stepped into SEMASHUR in 2016. Living in a boarding school was not easy. But luckily, my friends and teachers there were all supportive. I've created so many memories with the people I met there. SEMASHUR shaped me into who I am today, in one way or another. I will forever be grateful for the opportunity to study there.

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Merbok.
I'm currently studying for my Diploma in Library Informatics, in UiTM Merbok. It has been a fun experience so far, learning something different since I used to study Pure Sciences in high school. Being in the Library and Information Sciences Faculty has taught me alot about the importance of knowledge and information management, which I'm very proud of to be majoring in it.
