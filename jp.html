Recommended resources to study Japanese!

Helpful Websites


They're free and self-pace oriented!

Minato JF Website
Marugoto Online
These websites can be helpful if you want to start from scratch but at loss where to begin. They have lessons according to levels from beginner to advanced. The lessons are interactive and engaging, it's actually a wonder that they're free.

Study Blogs


From learners to fellow learners.

Nurul Rasya's Blog
Tofugu Learn Japanese
Blogs like these are made by fellow learners and teachers that know what students need especially in self-studying. They provide a lot of free resources and tips that are available online. You just need to have the will to read!

Textbooks


Classic, traditional, and very charming!

Minna no Nihongo Series
Marugoto Series
Textbooks are good for learning too as it delves deeper into the language. It is recommended to have a teacher though, because it can easily be demotivating to read a whole textbook by yourself. You can easily find these in Kinokuniya! The Marugoto textbooks are aligned with the online courses, too.
