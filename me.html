Who Am I?


On 16 February 2003, a baby girl was born.
She was supposed to be named as Putri Nur Maisarah, but the name ‘Maisarah’ was stolen by her older cousin instead, so her name became PUTRI NUR DALILAH. With her height no more than 145 cm, she is often called cute, which she really is. Putri is now a diploma student studying Library Informatics in UiTM Kedah. She’s a professional in daydreaming; her mind is always running, creating stories and conversations, or just thinking about her favourite characters from her favourite media. She’s currently into translating activities.
